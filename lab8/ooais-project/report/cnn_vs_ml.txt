CNN VS CLASSICAL ML
===================

CLASSICAL ML:
Model: Random Forest
Training time: 0.5074 s
Accuracy: 0.8611

CNN:
Model: Simple CNN
Training time: 1.4681 s
Accuracy: 0.8056

BETTER ACCURACY: Random Forest
FASTER TRAINING: Random Forest
FEWER CLASS CONFUSIONS: Random Forest
GENERALIZATION: Simple CNN
DIFFICULT IMAGES: Images with mixed land cover are the most difficult. Specifically, the CNN might struggle with river images that contain a lot of forest on the banks, as the convolutional filters pick up both textures.
MORE ROBUST: Simple CNN
IS COMPUTATIONAL COST OF CNN IS JUSTIFIED? In this specific case, not yet, but theoretically yes. For this small experiment, Random Forest is 3x faster and more accurate. However, the computational cost of a CNN is justified in real-world orbital systems because it scales better and it handles complexity.
